About me

I am a researcher at the natural resources institute Finland (Luke) and a PhD student at the university of Helsinki. My research studies sustainable business models and emergence of innovations and how those can help to solve challenges in steering towards sustainable development, in Finland and globally.

On this site, I aim to write about general topics related to my field of research, summary posts of my own research and sometimes about my other hobbies and interest.
